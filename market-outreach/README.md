# Market Outreach

Working folder for Ebrostay's go-to-market and outreach efforts. Ebrostay rents
furnished, move-in-ready homes for medium stays (1–12 months) in Zaragoza, Spain.

## Current focus

**Target segment #1 — Companies to rent out the existing furnished apartments.**

The goal is to place the four currently listed properties with corporate
tenants (relocation, project staff, interim management, training cohorts) who
need furnished medium-stay housing in Zaragoza. Companies are a high-value,
repeatable channel: one signed account can fill a unit for many months and
return for future placements.

- `research/` — market research inputs (the segmentation/target Excel goes here).
